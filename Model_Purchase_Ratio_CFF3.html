Granger Causality, Model Summary and Inferences :

Purchase Index: xt
ratio: yt
Since both these series are nonstationary we have transformed them first before the Granger Causality test.
Δxt = log(xt) − log (xt-1)
Δyt = log(𝑦t) − log (𝑦t-1)
xh = Δxt
yh = Δyt
